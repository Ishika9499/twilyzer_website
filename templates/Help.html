Where do i start from?

  1. Type a keyword into the input field, then click the Query button.


  2. Recent tweets that contain your keyword are pulled from Twitter.


  3. And, the analytical results deducted out of those tweets are visualized in the Sentiment tab, Emotion tab and the wordcloud tab.

Sentiment Tab

Line Chart

  1. The line chart shows the relation between the number of tweets (positive, negative and neutral) and the days they were posted.


Donut

  1. Donut represents the overall sentiment of the tweets fetched. It shows the percentage of tweets of each sentiment i.e. positive, negative and neutral.

Bar Graph

  1. The bar graph represents the percentage of different emotions such as fear, surprise, happiness and sadness of the tweets fetched their average polarity.

Word Cloud

  1. The word cloud, or tag cloud, is a textual data visualization which allows you to see in a single glance the words which have the highest frequency in all the tweets fetched. The importance of each word is shown with font size or color.
